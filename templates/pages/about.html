About SkillMatch Hub

Empowering Malaysian students to discover their perfect career path through data and psychology.

Our Mission

We aim to tackle the growing issue of degree mismatch and underemployment among fresh graduates. By identifying natural inclinations early, we help students make informed decisions before investing in their education.

How It Works

1

2

Analyze

3

Guide

Explore detailed career profiles, save your favorites, and compare them side-by-side to make the best choice.
